# 🏟️ StadiumIQ 2026 — Problem Statement

## Context

FIFA World Cup 2026 will be the largest sporting event in history — **48 teams, 104 matches, 16 host cities across USA, Canada, and Mexico**, with an estimated **5+ million in-person attendees** across the tournament.

Stadium operations at this scale are fundamentally complex. Staff must simultaneously manage:
- **68,000–94,000 fans per match** across venues ranging from MetLife Stadium to Estadio Azteca
- **Multi-lingual fan populations** from 48 countries speaking 50+ languages
- **Real-time crowd safety** with density fluctuations from pre-match arrival through post-match dispersal
- **Transport coordination** across rail, shuttle, bus, and rideshare networks serving venues with no dedicated parking for most fans
- **Accessibility requirements** for an estimated 3-5% of attendees requiring special assistance
- **Sustainability targets** — FIFA's commitment to carbon-neutral operations

## The Problem

> **Current stadium management systems are fragmented, reactive, and not AI-enabled.** Venue operations teams rely on radio communication, manual headcounts, and historical data — none of which scale to a 68,000-person, multi-lingual, multi-modal event happening every 3 days across 16 cities.

Specific pain points identified:

| Problem | Impact |
|---|---|
| No real-time crowd density visibility | Dangerous bottlenecks form before staff can respond |
| Manual language translation at info desks | Long queues, missed emergencies for non-English speakers |
| Reactive transport management | Post-match congestion lasting 60+ minutes |
| Fragmented accessibility services | Fans with disabilities experience delays and confusion |
| No integrated decision support | Venue directors lack cross-domain situational awareness |
| Manual sustainability tracking | Inconsistent reporting, missed green targets |

## The Solution: StadiumIQ 2026

StadiumIQ 2026 is a **GenAI-powered stadium intelligence platform** that transforms reactive, fragmented operations into **proactive, AI-guided, real-time management** through 8 integrated modules.
